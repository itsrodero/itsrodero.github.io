About TubeTools

TubeTools is a complete suite of free tools for YouTube creators. Our mission is to democratize access to analytics and optimization tools that were previously only available to large channels and agencies. All our tools are free, require no sign-up and have no usage limits.

The Thumbnail Extractor lets you download any YouTube thumbnail in HD 1080p instantly. The Thumbnail Analyzer gives you an instant CTR score, color palette and contrast analysis for any thumbnail you upload or analyze by URL.

The Title Generator creates ten high-CTR title variations for your topic using proven formulas based on real YouTube algorithm patterns. The Revenue Calculator estimates your earnings based on your views, RPM and niche.

Our guides cover everything you need to know about monetization: from YouTube Partner Program requirements to strategies for increasing your RPM and the highest-CPM niches in 2026. All content is based on real, verified data — not generic advice.
