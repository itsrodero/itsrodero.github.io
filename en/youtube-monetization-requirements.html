Getting monetized on YouTube is one of the most significant milestones for any creator. This guide covers every requirement, the exact application process, common reasons for rejection, and the most important steps to take immediately after approval to maximize your earnings.

The 5 Requirements to Join the YouTube Partner Program

👥 1,000 Subscribers on your channel
⏱️ 4,000 Watch hours in the last 12 months
📱 10M Shorts views (alternative to watch hours)
✅ 0 Active community guideline strikes
🏦 1 Linked Google AdSense account
💡 IMPORTANT DETAIL

The 4,000 watch hours must come from long-form videos only (not Shorts). Shorts views count toward a separate 10M view threshold. You can meet either the watch hours OR the Shorts views requirement — you don't need both.

YouTube's Two-Tier YPP System in 2026

YouTube has two levels of the Partner Program with different requirements and different monetization features available at each level:

Basic Tier

500 Subscribers + 3,000 Watch Hours

  • Channel Memberships
  • Super Thanks
  • Super Chat & Super Stickers
  • Shopping features
  • ❌ No AdSense ads
Full YPP

1,000 Subscribers + 4,000 Watch Hours

  • Everything in Basic
  • ✅ AdSense video ads
  • ✅ Display & overlay ads
  • ✅ Mid-roll ads (8+ min videos)
  • ✅ Full revenue reporting

Step-by-Step: How to Apply for Monetization

1

Verify your channel meets all requirements

Go to YouTube Studio → Monetization. You'll see a progress tracker showing your current subscriber count and watch hours versus the thresholds. All requirements must be met simultaneously.

2

Sign the YouTube Partner Program terms

In YouTube Studio → Monetization, click "Start" and review and accept the YPP terms of service. Read them carefully — violations of these terms after approval can result in removal from the program.

3

Create or link your Google AdSense account

You need a Google AdSense account linked to the same Google account as your YouTube channel. If you don't have one, YouTube will guide you through creating one. You must be 18+ or have a parent/guardian's account. One AdSense account can be linked to multiple YouTube channels.

4

Submit your channel for review

Once you've signed the terms and linked AdSense, your channel enters the review queue automatically. YouTube's team will review your content for advertiser-friendliness, policy compliance, and authenticity. This typically takes 1–4 weeks.

5

Wait for the email decision

You'll receive an email at your Google account email address. If approved: monetization is enabled immediately. If rejected: you'll see the reason and can reapply after 30 days once you've addressed the issues.

What to Do Immediately After Getting Approved

Approval is just the starting point. Most creators leave significant money on the table in the first weeks after monetization by not configuring their settings properly.

✅ POST-APPROVAL CHECKLIST

Enable all ad formats — skippable, non-skippable, bumper, overlay, display, and mid-roll ads. Set mid-roll ads to automatic placement on all videos over 8 minutes. Verify your AdSense payment address — payments are held until your address is verified. Set your content category correctly in channel settings — this affects which advertisers bid on your content. Review your highest-traffic videos and ensure they are all set to monetize.

How Long to Earn Your First $100 After Monetization

AdSense has a $100 minimum payout threshold. With a new monetized channel at typical early-stage view counts (500–2,000 views/month) and average RPM ($3–8), reaching the first payout can take 2–6 months. Channels in high-CPM niches (finance, software) can reach $100 faster with fewer views.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How many subscribers do you need to monetize YouTube in 2026?

You need 1,000 subscribers for the full YouTube Partner Program with AdSense ads. There is also a lower tier at 500 subscribers that unlocks channel memberships and Super Thanks, but not AdSense video ads.

How long does YouTube monetization approval take?

YouTube's monetization review typically takes 1 to 4 weeks in 2026. During busy periods it can take up to 6 weeks. You'll receive an email notification once the review is complete, whether approved or rejected.

Can you get monetized without 1,000 subscribers?

Partially. YouTube's lower-tier YPP at 500 subscribers unlocks channel memberships and Super Thanks revenue, but not AdSense display or video ads. Full ad monetization requires 1,000 subscribers and 4,000 watch hours.

What happens if YouTube rejects your monetization application?

You can reapply after 30 days. YouTube will show you the category of rejection (policy, content quality, etc.) so you can address the specific issue before reapplying. There's no limit to how many times you can reapply as long as you wait 30 days between attempts.
