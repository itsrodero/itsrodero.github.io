Our Commitment to Creators

The creator economy is growing faster than any other segment of the internet, yet most of the infrastructure built around it remains inaccessible to independent creators. Professional thumbnail testing tools cost hundreds of dollars per month. SEO optimization suites are built for brands, not individuals. Title research requires expensive keyword tools designed for traditional search engines, not YouTube's unique algorithm.

We built TubeTools to change that. Our commitment is simple: every tool on this site will always be free, always work without a sign-up, and always be built based on what creators actually need — not what's easiest to monetize. We measure our success by yours.

TubeTools is available in English and Spanish. All tools work in any language — simply paste a YouTube URL and get your results instantly. Our guides are written by creators who have studied YouTube analytics extensively and base every recommendation on published YouTube Creator Academy material, AdSense documentation, and independently verified channel growth data.

Why we chose ad funding over subscriptions: We considered several business models before launching TubeTools. A freemium model would mean putting the most useful features behind a paywall — which defeats the purpose. A donation model is unpredictable and hard to sustain. Ad funding through Google AdSense lets us serve creators who cannot afford paid tools while keeping the business sustainable. Every page view helps us keep the lights on. We are transparent about this and we appreciate every creator who uses TubeTools.

Our Tools in Detail

The Thumbnail Extractor works by accessing YouTube's public image CDN, which stores thumbnails at multiple resolutions for every video. We present these in HD 1080p (maxresdefault), 720p (hqdefault), and 480p (mqdefault). The download happens directly in your browser with no data passing through our servers.

The CTR Score Calculator scores your thumbnail on 12 criteria that research shows correlate with higher click-through rates: color contrast, face presence, text quantity, text legibility, emotional resonance, composition, mobile visibility, brand consistency, niche alignment, curiosity gap, value proposition clarity, and competitive differentiation.

The Revenue Calculator uses publicly documented YouTube Partner Program payment structures and real RPM data gathered from creator disclosures across different niches and regions. Finance and investment channels average $15–40 RPM. Gaming channels average $3–8 RPM. Educational content sits at $8–18 RPM depending on audience demographics and geography.
