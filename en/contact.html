Frequently Asked Questions

Are all TubeTools tools really free?

Yes. Every tool on TubeTools is 100% free with no sign-up required. We monetize through Google AdSense advertising, which allows us to keep everything free for creators.

How do I download a YouTube thumbnail in HD?

Go to our Thumbnail Extractor, paste the YouTube video URL, click Analyze, and download the 1080p version. No account needed.

Do you store my data?

No. We don't store the URLs you analyze or the images you download. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Can I suggest a new tool?

Absolutely. Use the form above or email us directly. We build tools based on what creators actually need, and community feedback drives our roadmap.

About Our Tools and Privacy

TubeTools processes all thumbnail analysis directly in your browser. When you paste a YouTube URL, we fetch the thumbnail directly from YouTube's public CDN — no data is stored on our servers. When you upload an image to the Thumbnail Analyzer, that image is processed locally in your browser using JavaScript and is never transmitted to any server.

We use Google AdSense to display relevant advertising. Google may use cookies to show personalized ads based on your browsing history. You can opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ad Settings. Our full privacy policy explains exactly what data is collected and how it is used.

We are available in English and Spanish. Our entire tool suite and article library is available in both languages. If you find a translation error or have a suggestion to improve our Spanish or English content, please let us know through the form above — we take content quality seriously.
