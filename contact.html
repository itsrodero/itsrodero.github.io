Frequently Asked Questions about TubeTools

Is TubeTools completely free?

Yes, and it will stay that way. The project is funded through non-intrusive Google AdSense advertising. There is no premium tier or paid subscription. All tools are fully accessible without creating an account.

Why doesn't the download work on my browser?

On iOS/Safari, there are restrictions on directly downloading images from external domains. Try long-pressing on the thumbnail and selecting "Save Image." On desktop, if the issue persists, write to me with your browser name and version.

Do you store the URLs I enter?

No. TubeTools does not store the URLs you enter or the images you download. Everything is processed in real time. We only collect anonymous visit analytics with prior consent. See the Privacy Policy for full details.

Why doesn't the HD 1080p version always appear?

YouTube only generates the 1080p version for videos uploaded in high resolution. For videos uploaded in standard quality or very old videos, the maximum available resolution will be 720p or 480p. This is a YouTube limitation, not a TubeTools issue.

Can I suggest a blog article?

Absolutely. If there's a topic about YouTube, monetization, thumbnails, or CTR that isn't covered in the blog and you think would help other creators, send me the suggestion via the form above. Several of the best articles came from community requests.

What is the response time?

I personally read and reply to every message within 24 to 48 hours on business days. During busier periods (especially Q4, October–December), it may take up to 72 hours. No message goes unanswered.
